About us

Started in a little log cabin in the deeps woods of Michigan, we have been hand crafting guitar parts for over 20 years. Using only the finest materials, and techniques passed along through generations of woodworkers and luthiers, we pride ourselves in creating some of the most intimately detailed and personal craftwork that you will ever find in musical instruments.
